Game Rules
  • Rock beats Scissor, Scissor beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock
  • Agree ahead of time whether you will count off "Rock, Paper, Scissor, shoot" of just "Rock, Paper, Scissor"
  • User Rock, Paper, Scissor to settle minor decisions or simply play to pass the time
  • If both Players lay down the same hand, each Player lays down another hand
